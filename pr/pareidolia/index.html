From CTC Studios and the WGBH Podcast Network. The Black Tapes is in part an exploration of belief and the search for truth, and in part a profile of the founder of the Gottlieb Institute, professional skeptic Dr. Hermann Gottlieb. Hosted by Newt Geiszler and produced by Mako Mori. Follow us on Twitter @blacktapespod.
